Reading Notes & Book Reviews

书是随身携带的花园。在这里记录我读过的文字与思考。
"A book is like a garden carried in the pocket." My collection of thoughts and readings.

《置身内外部》

Interiors and Exteriors

★★★★★

这本书不仅仅是在讨论设计，它更像是在拆解我们观察世界的方式。作者通过极其理性的逻辑，揭示了创意背后的秩序感。

This book is more than just a discussion on design; it’s an exploration of how we perceive the world. The author reveals the sense of order behind creativity through impeccable logic.

《艺术的故事》

The Story of Art

★★★★★

作为艺术史的入门经典，贡布里希将宏大的历史理得极为顺滑。它教会我，艺术不是孤立的产物，而是时代灵魂的写照。

As a classic introduction to art history, Gombrich weaves a grand narrative with incredible smoothness. It taught me that art is not an isolated product, but a reflection of the era's soul.
